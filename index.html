Museum-eyes Game

hide instructions
  1. Share your names and pronouns.
  2. Decide what order you will share in.
  3. Pick one person to share their screen.
  4. Click "pick a card."
  5. Read the card aloud. (Anyone can read aloud.)
  6. Go around the circle, giving everyone an opportunity to respond to the card.
  7. Pick a new card and repeat until you're brought back to the main room.
